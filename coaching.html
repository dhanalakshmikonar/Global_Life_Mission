Coaching for Life

Coaching is a development process where a trained professional, the coach, supports a learner, the coachee, in achieving personal or professional goals through training, guidance, and structured dialogue, such as asking powerful questions to facilitate the coachee's self-discovery and problem-solving. The focus is on empowering individuals to find their own solutions and unlock their potential, leading to growth, improved performance, and increased self-confidence.

Key Characteristics of Coaching

  1. • Goal-Oriented:
    • The process is structured around specific, achievable personal or professional goals.
  2. • Collaborative Partnership:
    • It's a partnership between a coach and coachee, fostering growth and development.
  3. • Empowerment:
    • The coach empowers the coachee to find their own solutions rather than providing direct answers.
  4. • Future-Oriented:
    • The focus is on the coachee's future aspirations and desired outcomes.
  5. • Skill-Focused:
    • It involves training and developing skills for enhanced productivity or to overcome performance issues.
