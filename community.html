Good Life Community

Community works focuses on improving the overall well-being of a community by addressing systemic social issues, such as addictions, human trafficking, and lack of awareness.

It involves collaboration with community members, local organizations, and government agencies to develop programs, implement policies, and advocate for social justice.

Key strategies include community organizing, empowerment, capacity building, and connecting residents with services and support systems to create positive, lasting change within the community.

Children’s Home for Boys and Girls (Juvenile Welfare Services)

Child welfare services provide support to children and families through services like prevention, rehabilitation, foster care, and adoption, aiming to protect children from harm and promote their overall development. In India, programs like the Integrated Child Development Services (ICDS) and the Integrated Child Protection Scheme (ICPS), alongside the universal child helpline number 1098, support children in need.

Vigilance Home – Women’s Empowerment

Women's empowerment is the process and ability for women to gain control over their own lives and destinies by making their own choices, improving their sense of self-worth, and gaining equal opportunities in all societal spheres. It involves women becoming aware of and challenging gender-based inequalities to achieve a just and equitable society where they can participate fully in social, economic, and political life and influence positive change.

Additional Core Values

  1. Preventive, Promotive, and Rehabilitative Care
    • They offer services to families and children to prevent difficulties, promote well-being, and provide care for children in need.
  2. Child Protection
    • This includes services for children in need of care and protection, such as those who are orphaned, destitute, or in conflict with the law.
  3. Support for Vulnerable Children
    • Services focus on children from poverty groups, children of working mothers, and handicapped children.
  4. Self-Worth
    • Fostering women's belief in themselves and their right to make decisions.
  5. Agency & Choice
    • Granting women the freedom to determine their own lives and make choices without external restrictions.
  6. Access to Opportunities
    • Providing equal access to education, training, resources, and livelihoods.
  7. Control Over Lives
    • Empowering women to control their lives both inside and outside the home.
  8. Challenging Inequality
    • Enabling women to identify and challenge social, economic, and political injustices.
  9. Influence & Participation
    • Ensuring women have a voice and can participate in and influence decisions that affect them and society.
