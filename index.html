About Us

Good Life Mission International

Christ is our message!
We preach to awaken hearts and bring every person into the full understanding of truth. Our goal is to bring people into maturity and live boldly for Christ.
Led by Rajan Kaliraj Arumugam, Good Life Mission helps believers discover their identity in Christ, recognize God as a loving Father, and live empowered by the Holy Spirit.
Grace Yamunadevi, his wife, “proverbs:31” woman, born to a missionary couple is a pillar of support to Rajan. Her humility, compassion and simplicity have led her to counsel and bring God’s healing to many people. Rajan and Grace are blessed with two children.

Through our initiatives, we support families, create opportunities, and strengthen values that nurture a Happy, Healthy, and Holistic life.

Our Journey

Established with faith and vision, Good Life Mission began its journey of service by reaching out to those in need. Today, our programs focus on spiritual guidance, community development, and welfare through practical solutions.

Meet Good Life Mission International

Living with Purpose, Guided by Hope, and Strengthened by Faith

Good Life Mission exists to uplift individuals and families through the power of faith. We believe in building stronger communities by sharing the message of hope, extending compassion, and encouraging a life rooted in purpose and love.

  • ✔ Grounded in prayer and faith in Christ.
  • ✔ Dedicated to spiritual growth and discipleship.
  • ✔ Living and teaching the Good Life through God’s grace.

How We Make a Difference

Guiding Hearts, Transforming Lives

At Good Life Mission, we walk with individuals and communities on a journey rooted in faith and filled with purpose. Through active outreach, spiritual mentoring, and compassionate fellowship, we nurture growth, ignite hope, and inspire lives renewed in grace.

  • We foster personal transformation through prayer, teaching, and discipleship.
  • We build vibrant communities anchored in love, service, and unity.
  • We empower people to live with purpose, anchored in faith and hope.
