Church Ministry – Good Life Mission

Christ is our message!
We preach to awaken hearts and bring every person into the full understanding of truth. Our goal is to bring people into maturity and live boldly for Christ.

Led by Rajan Kaliraj Arumugam, Good Life Mission helps believers discover their identity in Christ, recognize God as a loving Father, and live empowered by the Holy Spirit.

Through worship, teaching, and discipleship, the Church Ministry nurtures spiritual growth, builds strong communities, and spreads the message of hope and salvation.

Grace Yamunadevi, his wife, “proverbs:31” woman, born to a missionary couple is a pillar of support to Rajan. Her humility, compassion and simplicity have led her to counsel and bring God’s healing to many people. Rajan and Grace are blessed with two children.

7 Core Values

  1. Connection
    • Practicing vulnerability to facilitate belonging.
    • Combating shame, fear and anxiety.
    • Holding stories as holy ground.
  2. Honor
    • We cannot receive what we don’t honor.
    • How we honor while we disagree & confront.
    • How are my thoughts and conversations in someone’s absence?
  3. Supernatural
    • Being aware and expecting a supernatural solution at all times.
    • Being authentic in the stories we share.
    • Moving in Wisdom and Power.
  4. Presence
    • Being sensitive to the still small voice.
    • Choosing to spend time in Prayer and Bible meditation.
    • Increasing awareness of the presence at challenging times.
  5. Generosity
    • Being generous in thoughts & interpretation of others’ intent.
    • Being financially generous towards the big picture.
    • Being generous towards the needs.
  6. Freedom
    • Free to say ‘yes’ or ‘no’ and mean it.
    • Free of offense and free to keep our love on.
    • Free to explore and take risks and own the mess to clean it.
  7. Legacy
    • Making choices considering generations to come.
    • Constantly moving towards excellence.
    • Making our personal victories a corporate breakthrough.

Good Life Mission is dedicated to spreading hope, faith, and love. Together, we build stronger communities and inspire lives through the Word of God.
